ॐ नमः शिवाय शिवाय नमः ॐ

Shiva Panchakshara Stotram

By Sri Adi Shankaracharya

ॐ नमः शिवाय शिवाय नमः ॐ
ॐ नमः शिवाय शिवाय नमः ॐ

Translation:
Om. Salutations to Shiva. Salutations again to the auspicious Lord Shiva. May the whole mind bow into the presence of Shiva.

॥ १ ॥

नागेन्द्रहाराय त्रिलोचनाय
भस्माङ्गरागाय महेश्वराय ।
नित्याय शुद्धाय दिगंबराय
तस्मै “न” काराय नमः शिवाय ॥

Translation:
Salutations to Shiva in the syllable "na": the great eternal and pure Lord, three-eyed, garlanded with the king of serpents, adorned with sacred ash, and clothed only in the directions.

॥ २ ॥

मन्दाकिनी सलिल चन्दन चर्चिताय
नन्दीश्वर प्रमथनाथ महेश्वराय ।
मन्दार मुख्य बहुपुष्प सुपूजिताय
तस्मै “म” काराय नमः शिवाय ॥

Translation:
Salutations to Shiva in the syllable "ma": the great Lord worshipped with Ganga water, sandal paste, and many flowers; the Lord of Nandi and the divine attendants.

॥ ३ ॥

शिवाय गौरी वदनाब्ज बृन्द
सूर्याय दक्षाध्वर नाशकाय ।
श्री नीलकण्ठाय वृषभध्वजाय
तस्मै “शि” काराय नमः शिवाय ॥

Translation:
Salutations to Shiva in the syllable "śi": the auspicious Lord who is like the sun to the lotus-face of Gauri, who destroyed Daksha's sacrifice, who bears the blue throat, and whose banner is the bull.

॥ ४ ॥

वशिष्ठ कुम्भोद्भव गौतमाद्य
मुनीन्द्र देवार्चित शेखराय ।
चन्द्रार्क वैश्वानर लोचनाय
तस्मै “व” काराय नमः शिवाय ॥

Translation:
Salutations to Shiva in the syllable "va": the crowned Lord worshipped by great sages and gods, whose three eyes are the moon, the sun, and fire.

॥ ५ ॥

यज्ञस्वरूपाय जटाधराय
पिनाकहस्ताय सनातनाय ।
दिव्याय देवाय दिगंबराय
तस्मै “य” काराय नमः शिवाय ॥

Translation:
Salutations to Shiva in the syllable "ya": the eternal, radiant Lord whose form is sacrifice, who bears matted locks, who holds the Pinaka bow, and who is clothed in the directions.

पञ्चाक्षरमिदं पुण्यं यः पठेच्छिव सन्निधौ ।
शिवलोकमवाप्नोति शिवेन सह मोदते ॥

Phalashruti (Benefits):
Whoever recites this sacred five-syllabled hymn in Shiva's presence attains the realm of Shiva and rejoices with Shiva.

ॐ नमः शिवाय ॐ

May Lord Shiva bless you with peace and devotion.
